Refining Services

We provide refining services for many large clients through Western Canada. Our experienced staff can be trusted and provide you with confidence in your final melt value.

Our refining service process is accurete, fast, secure and simple.

Our fast settlement process provides you with the cash flow you need which enables you to integrate that lifeblood into your business or personal life.

Step 1

Collect Metal

Bring in your Gold, Silver, or other Precious Metals to our head-office in Metro Vancouver. We also offer a hassle-free fully insured mail-in service for clients outside of Metro Vancouver or British Columbia.

Step 2

Melting

Our experienced team will then take your metals, melting them into liquid form and putting them into moulds to form bar(s).

Step 3

Testing

Once the melting has completed, our team will then conduct tests to determine the percentages of gold or silver or other previous metals within melted bar(s).

Step 4

Payment

Once we have analysed the precious metal bar(s), we pay you via Cheque, Wire Transfer or in Bullion Products such as Gold, Silver, Platinum Bars & Coins.

Our order process is fast, convenient and secure.
